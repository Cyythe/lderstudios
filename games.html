Grymwar: Online

For many centuries, the world of Grymwar has been in constant turmoil caused by seven deities that each vie for control. Kingdoms rise and fall, cataclysmic events occur with ever increasing frequency, and common people suffer as their lands are ravaged by famine, greed, war, and magic. To tip the scales in their favor, each deity seeks to find champions to rally the lands and bring their agenda to bear. The choices and actions of each champion will impact the world on a scale so grand that it is incomprehensible. How drastically will they change the world of Grymwar? Only time will tell.

Global Impact

  • Choices cumulatively impact the world wide influence of each deity as you play. Players can gain great favor or displeasure from deities as they wax or wane in power.

Play as You Want

  • Grymwar: Online lets players create their characters how they want. With no class system, a player can choose to become a great warrior, a cunning politician, or even a deadly assassin. Choose skills that cater to your play style and change the world how you see fit.

Collaborate with Friends or Go it Alone

  • Find like-minded players online to achieve your lofty goals or venture forth as a loner.

Divine Intervention

  • As you play Grymwar: Online, deities may attempt to intervene for or against champions to turn the tide in the favor.

RITE

A priest, sent to a small town to perform the rite of exorcism, soon discovers that what is 'right' is a matter of perspective. Playing as Father Salvatore and armed with only the horrific truths disclosed by an evil spirit, you must unravel mysteries that have been closely guarded by his organization for untold years. Who can you trust? How will you know the truth?
